Airbnb Visualization - Price Exploration

Airbnb is an online marketplace that connects people who want to rent out their homes with people who are looking for accommodations in that locale. It is currently available in more than 81000 cities and 191 countries worldwide and is a viable option for visitors as it helps them connect and stay with the locals at a reasonable price. Staying in an Airbnb offers a lot of advantages over traditional hotels for prospective travellers to that locale. Airbnb are on an average cheaper than some of the high tier traditional hotels, while offering amenities of the same level. Airbnb also provides an opportunity to travellers to experience and understand the local culture better though their Airbnb hosts, who are often locals themselves.

Correlation Matrix

A correlation matrix is a table showing correlation coefficients between variables. Each cell in the table shows the correlation between the two variables.

By clicking on the cell you can change the below 2 plots, to get a better feel of how the attributes are correlated.

By hovering over the cells we can see the correlation value between the attributes.

Positive correlation are in green, while negative correlation are in blue.

Scatter Plot:

We use this plot to determine if there is any type of relation between our selected attributes. For a few attributes we might see a linear, exponential or logarithmic relation between the data of our attributes. Hovering over a point gives us the x and y value of the point.

Histogram:

We use this diagram to get a better understanding of the distribution of our data. We can get a quick overview of how our data is segregated among different ranges. We can change the bin size using the provided input box. Hovering over the bar gives us the tid-bits about the selected range.
